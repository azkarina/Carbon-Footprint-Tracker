About SDG 13

Sustainable Development Goal 13 aims to take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. Human activities are estimated to have caused approximately 1.0°C of global warming above pre-industrial levels.

Did you know? The carbon footprint of the average person is about 4 tons per year. To avoid a 2℃ rise in global temperatures, we need to reduce this to under 2 tons by 2050.
